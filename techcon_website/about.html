History

TechCon was established in 2010 with the goal of creating a platform for innovators, tech enthusiasts, and industry leaders to share knowledge and drive technological advancements.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and connect individuals through technology, fostering innovation and collaboration to address real-world challenges.

Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

A pioneer in computer programming, Dr. Lovelace shared insights into the evolution of programming languages and their future implications.

Mr. Alan Turing

Known as the father of modern computing, Mr. Turing's keynote speech highlighted the potential of AI in solving complex problems.

Ms. Grace Hopper

A trailblazer in software development, Ms. Hopper shared her experiences in the early days of coding and her vision for the tech industry.
